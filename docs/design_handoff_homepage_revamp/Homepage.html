FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is a Web3 development company?
A Web3 development company designs, audits, and ships blockchain products: DeFi protocols, smart contracts, NFT marketplaces, and on-chain integrations. Metaborong is one such studio: three technical co-founders, Web3 and AI as equal practices, with crypto-native teams and founders as the typical clients.
How long does it take to build a DeFi protocol or smart contract?
A working DeFi protocol or audited smart contract ships in four to eight weeks when one senior team owns architecture, engineering, and deployment continuously. Multichain or audit-heavy work may extend into 10–12 weeks, but the spec stays locked and the team doesn't change mid-build.
Are Web3 and AI development projects priced hourly or fixed-scope?
Metaborong defaults to fixed-scope per engagement so the timeline and the deliverables are knowable upfront. Hourly retainers are available for ongoing maintenance, audits, and post-launch iteration. Equity arrangements are considered for early-stage founders building alongside us.
What is the difference between a Web3 agency and a smaller development studio?
A large Web3 agency optimises for procurement maturity, account management layers, and multi-quarter engagements. A small studio like Metaborong optimises for founder-level access to the engineers writing the code, faster decisions, and shorter time-to-production. Both have a place; the right choice depends on what the project actually needs.
What is an AI agent development company?
An AI agent development company builds production agents: RAG pipelines, agentic workflows, copilots, conversational assistants, and generative systems that hold up under real user load. Metaborong ships agents into existing products and new ones — including SunsetML's writing tool and PredictRAM's analyst stack.
Do you need an NDA before discussing a Web3 or AI project?
No NDA is required for an initial conversation. We sign mutual NDAs once we are deeper into spec, architecture, or anything touching tokenomics or proprietary data. The first conversation is about whether we are the right team for what you are building, not about extracting your secrets.
What is a custom SaaS product development studio?
A custom SaaS product development studio builds full products end-to-end: discovery, architecture, frontend, backend, infrastructure, and deployment. Metaborong's Product Studio works with founders without a CTO, shipping MVPs, SaaS, and B2B multi-tenant platforms inside the same 4–12 week engagement model.
How do lean Web3 and AI development studios ship as fast as larger agencies?
Lean studios skip the layers. One senior team owns the architecture, the engineering, and the deployment continuously — no re-bidding talent across phases, no handing off across vendor boundaries. Combined with agentic AI inside our own spec, review, and QA workflows, the cycle from decision to shipped code is short by design.
