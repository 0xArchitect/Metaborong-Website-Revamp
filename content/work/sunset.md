## The Problem

Writers who use AI every day work against tools that were never built for them. They switch constantly between a writing editor and an external AI chatbot, lose document context with every copy-paste cycle, and fight cluttered interfaces that compete for attention during deep work. Most AI writing tools keep no memory of the document between prompts and offer no real support for long-form, multi-session projects.

The root cause is that traditional editors were optimized for formatting, not for writing flow — the state where ideas move faster than the friction of the tools around them. The hard problem is not bolting a chatbot onto a text box. It is designing a writing environment where intelligent assistance feels like a natural extension of thought rather than an interruption to it.

## What Metaborong Built

Metaborong engineered SunsetML as a contextual AI writing system built to keep writers in flow, hold their focus, and strip friction from the work. AI lives inside the writing session, not in a separate window: a Contextual PromptBar, live multi-model switching, an adaptive editing environment, real-time collaboration, attachment-aware prompting, and structured workspace management. Metaborong's scope spanned product design, AI integration, and full-stack engineering — the full build.

### The Contextual AI PromptBar

The centerpiece is the Contextual PromptBar, an in-flow AI layer embedded directly in the writing session. Instead of opening a separate AI window, a writer highlights text in the editor and acts on it in place. It handles rewriting and paraphrasing, summarization, ideation, custom per-document tone control, and slash-command shortcuts. Attachment-aware processing feeds PDFs and documents straight into prompts, so research never leaves the editor.

### Live Multi-Model Switching

SunsetML runs multi-model AI orchestration across GPT-5.5, GPT-5.4 mini, Claude Sonnet 4.6, and Claude Opus 4.7. Writers pick the right model for each task — a fast model for quick edits, a frontier model for hard reasoning — and switch dynamically mid-session without breaking context or rebuilding their prompt environment.

### Adaptive Writing Environment

SunsetML's writing environment adapts to each session in real time: dynamic UI theming shifts with writing context, focus-preservation modes minimize visual noise during deep work, and editing controls appear only when needed. Unlike static editors, every element that does not serve active writing stays hidden until it is called.

### Real-Time Collaboration Infrastructure

SunsetML gives long-form projects a full collaboration layer. Workspaces and folders organize drafts, research, and publishing pipelines. Teams co-edit in real time with live cursor presence, comment and annotate in the document, and share with permission-based access for clients. The result is a production-ready writing platform for content agencies, editorial teams, and distributed creators.

## Technical Approach

Metaborong built SunsetML on Next.js and React for the editor surface, a NestJS backend for AI orchestration and collaboration services, and Framer Motion for the adaptive, low-noise interface. Delivering real-time AI assistance inside a live editor meant solving several problems at once: low-latency response delivery, real-time collaborative synchronization, contextual routing across multiple model providers, and uninterrupted typing performance while AI operations run.

The result is a contextual routing layer that keeps AI assistance invisible until it is needed and near-instant when it is called — fast enough that switching between GPT-5.5, GPT-5.4 mini, Claude Sonnet 4.6, and Claude Opus 4.7 never interrupts the writing session.
