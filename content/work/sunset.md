## Overview

Most writing software was built for formatting documents, not for thinking at speed.

SUNSET is an AI-native writing platform engineered for creators, researchers, content teams, and high-frequency writers who rely on AI throughout every stage of the creative process. Unlike conventional editors that treat AI as a bolt-on feature, SUNSET was purpose-built around a single principle: intelligent assistance should feel native to the writing session, not external to it.

Metaborong partnered with SUNSET to design and engineer the complete platform from the ground up. The result is a production-grade AI writing assistant where writing, AI interaction, real-time collaboration, and workflow management exist inside one continuous, distraction-free environment built for individual writers and collaborative teams alike.

## The Problem

Writers who use AI daily face a consistent set of friction points that existing tools fail to solve. They switch constantly between writing editors and external AI tools, lose context with every copy-paste cycle, and work inside cluttered interfaces that compete for attention during deep work sessions. Most AI writing tools have no memory of the document context between interactions and offer no real support for long-form, multi-session projects.

Traditional document editors were optimized for formatting. SUNSET needed to be optimized for writing flow, the mental state where ideas move faster than the friction of the tools surrounding them. The engineering challenge was not adding an AI chatbot alongside a text editor. It was designing a writing environment where intelligent assistance feels like a natural extension of thought rather than an interruption to it.

## What Metaborong Built

Metaborong engineered SUNSET as a contextual AI writing system centered around workflow continuity, cognitive focus preservation, and low-friction creative production.

The core platform includes a Contextual AI PromptBar as the primary in-editor AI interaction layer, an adaptive writing environment that responds to the nature of the writing session, multi-model AI orchestration with dynamic switching between AI providers, real-time collaboration infrastructure for co-editing and shared workspaces, attachment-aware AI prompting that processes documents and PDFs inline, and structured workspace management with scalable folder and project architecture.

### The Contextual AI PromptBar

The centerpiece of the platform is the Contextual PromptBar, an in-flow AI interaction layer embedded directly into the writing session. Instead of opening a separate AI window, writers interact with AI by highlighting text inside the editor. The PromptBar supports rewriting and paraphrasing, summarization, ideation, tone refinement, slash command shortcuts, and attachment-aware processing that feeds PDFs and documents directly into prompts without leaving the editor.

SUNSET supports multi-model switching across OpenAI GPT models, Claude by Anthropic, and Google Gemini. Writers can choose the right model for the right task and switch dynamically without breaking context or rebuilding their prompt environment.

### Adaptive Writing Environment

Most writing interfaces remain visually static regardless of the writer's intent or focus state. SUNSET's adaptive writing environment responds subtly to the nature of each session. Dynamic UI theming adjusts based on writing tone and context. Focus-preservation modes minimize visual noise during deep work. Editing controls appear contextually and disappear when not in use. Every element that does not serve active writing is hidden until it is needed.

For writers in India and globally working across long research sessions, creative projects, or publishing workflows, this approach directly results in longer, more productive writing sessions with fewer tool interruptions.

### Real-Time Collaboration Infrastructure

Long-form writing projects require organizational infrastructure that most AI writing tools do not provide. SUNSET includes scalable workspace and folder systems for drafts, research, and publishing pipelines, real-time collaborative editing with live cursor presence, in-document commenting and annotation, and permission-based sharing controls for team and client workflows. This makes SUNSET a production-ready collaborative writing software platform for content agencies, editorial teams, and distributed creator teams.
